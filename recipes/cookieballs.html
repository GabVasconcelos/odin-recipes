Cookie balls

Description

Easy recipe for cookie balls with only three ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1lb of chocolate sandwich cookies, crushed
  • 1 (8oz) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1lb of vanilla flavored candy coating, melted

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine crushed cookies and cream cheese to form a stiff dough.
  2. Roll into balls and dip with a fork in melted candy coating
  3. Let on waxed paper until set.
